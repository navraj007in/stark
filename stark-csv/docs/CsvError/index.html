CsvError

pub enum CsvError

Typed CSV parse/write errors. Every offset and index is zero-based.

  • InputTooLarge(actual_bytes, limit) -- the input is larger than CsvLimits::max_input_bytes.
  • TooManyRecords(attempted_records, limit) -- more records than CsvLimits::max_records.
  • TooManyFields(record, attempted_fields, limit) -- more fields in one record than

CsvLimits::max_fields_per_record.

  • TooManyTotalFields(attempted_total_fields, limit) -- more fields across the whole document

than CsvLimits::max_total_fields.

  • FieldTooLarge(record, field, attempted_bytes, limit) -- a decoded field exceeds

CsvLimits::max_field_bytes.

  • QuoteInUnquotedField(offset, record, field) -- a quote byte appeared after unquoted field

content; a quote only opens a quoted field as the field's first byte.

  • UnexpectedAfterClosingQuote(offset, record, field, byte) -- a byte other than

comma/LF/CRLF/EOF followed a closing quote.

  • BareCarriageReturn(offset, record, field) -- a bare CR (not followed by LF) appeared

outside a quoted field.

  • UnterminatedQuotedField(opening_quote_offset, record, field) -- input ended while a quoted

field was still open.

  • OutputTooLarge(attempted_bytes, limit) -- serialized output exceeds

CsvLimits::max_output_bytes.
